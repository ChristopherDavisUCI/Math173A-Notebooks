from Helper1 import ind_co, get_freq

The ind_co function estimates the index of coincidence. (Warning. Itâ€™s not exactly the same as in the textbook.) The index of coincidence gives the probability that two randomly chosen characters are the same character. For random English characters, we expect a probability of about 1/26 approx 0.038. (Higher for shorter strings.) For actual English, we expect a probability of higher, about 0.06.

Textbook/usual definition does random selection without replacement. My function ind_co uses replacement.
